About Bijaylaxmi

I hold dual master's degrees in Atmospheric Sciences and Oceanography, complemented by a bachelor's in Mathematics. With over 5 years of experience, I specialize in Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

My expertise lies in large data handling, numerical modeling, and programming in MATLAB and Python. I am an enthusiastic learner, capable of adapting to new areas of knowledge, and I possess strong oral and written communication skills.

Beyond research, I am a skilled drone operator and scientific vessel captain. I enjoy learning about history, geography, and new languages.

Key Projects

Featured Project

20-Year Assessment of TSS Variability

A comprehensive study analyzing the spatiotemporal variability of Total Suspended Sediment (TSS) in Barataria Bay, USA, from 2000 to 2020 using MODIS Aqua satellite data.

Ocean Color Remote Sensing Python/MATLAB

High-Res LULC Mapping

ArcGIS Urban Planning

COVID-19 SEIR Modeling

Implemented the SEIR mathematical model to simulate the COVID-19 outbreak in India and estimate the basic reproduction number ($R_0$).

MATLAB Epidemiology

Wave Data Gap Filling (ANN)
